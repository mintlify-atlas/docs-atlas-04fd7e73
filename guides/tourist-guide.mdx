## Welcome, Tourist!

Kin Conecta connects you with verified local guides who offer authentic, personalized experiences. This guide will help you make the most of the platform.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Navigate to the registration page and select **Turista** as your account type. You'll need to provide:
    - Full name
    - Date of birth
    - Country code and phone number
    - Email address
    - Password (minimum 8 characters)
    
    <Note>
      You must be 18 or older to create an account on Kin Conecta.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    After registration, you'll be guided through a profile wizard to personalize your experience. This helps guides understand your interests and preferences.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    Check your inbox for a verification email to activate your account and access all features.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Exploring Experiences

The **Explorar** page is your gateway to discovering amazing local experiences.

### Browse and Search

- Use the **search bar** at the top to search by destination, guide name, or activity type
- Click the **Buscar** button to execute your search
- Browse through experience cards showing tour titles, locations, ratings, and prices

### Filter by Category

Use the filter buttons to narrow down experiences:

- **Todos** - View all available experiences
- **Gastronomía** - Food tours and culinary experiences
- **Aventura** - Outdoor and adventure activities
- **Historia** - Historical tours and cultural heritage
- **Cultura** - Art, traditions, and cultural immersion

<Tip>
  Click on any category filter to instantly see relevant experiences. The active filter is highlighted for easy reference.
</Tip>

### Understanding Experience Cards

Each experience card displays:

- **Tour title** and description
- **Location** with a location pin icon
- **Category tags** showing tour themes
- **Star rating** from previous tourists
- **Price per person** in MXN
- **High-quality photo** of the experience

## Managing Favorites

Save experiences you're interested in for later review.

### Adding to Favorites

1. Click the heart icon on any experience card while browsing
2. The experience is instantly saved to your **Favoritos** page
3. You can favorite both guides and specific experiences

### Viewing Your Favorites

Navigate to **Favoritos** from the sidebar to access your saved items:

- Switch between **Guías** and **Experiencias** tabs
- Each tab shows a count of saved items
- Use the search bar to quickly find specific favorites
- Remove items by clicking the heart icon again

<Note>
  Your favorites are private and only visible to you. Save as many as you like to build your dream itinerary!
</Note>

## Managing Your Trips

The **Mis viajes** page helps you track all your bookings in one place.

### Upcoming Trips

Your next confirmed booking is featured prominently with:

- Tour title and featured image
- Location and date
- Guide information with profile photo
- Quick action buttons:
  - **Ver detalles** - View full booking details
  - **Contactar guía** - Open chat with your guide

### Trip History

Below your featured trip, you'll find your complete booking history including:

- Past completed tours
- Upcoming bookings
- Cancelled reservations

Each trip shows the tour name, date, guide, and current status.

## Your Profile

Access your profile from the sidebar to:

- View and edit your personal information
- Update your profile photo
- Manage your contact preferences
- Review your booking history
- See reviews you've written

### Editing Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Profile Settings">
    Click **Tu perfil** in the sidebar, then click **Actualizar información**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update Information">
    Edit your name, contact details, or preferences as needed
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click the save button to update your profile
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Notifications

Stay informed about your bookings and messages:

- Click the **bell icon** in the top-right corner to view notifications
- A red dot indicates unread notifications
- Notifications include booking confirmations, guide messages, and trip reminders

<Tip>
  Enable browser notifications to receive real-time updates about your tours and messages from guides.
</Tip>

## Safety and Support

### Verified Guides

Look for the **verified badge** on guide profiles, which indicates:

- Identity verification completed
- Background check passed
- Required certifications uploaded

### Getting Help

If you need assistance:

1. Navigate to **Ayuda** in the sidebar
2. Browse frequently asked questions
3. Contact support directly through the help center
4. Use the chat widget to message guides with questions
