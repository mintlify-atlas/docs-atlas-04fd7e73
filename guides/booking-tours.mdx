## Overview

Booking a tour on Kin Conecta is quick and straightforward. This guide walks you through the entire process from discovering an experience to confirming your booking.

## Before You Book

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create and Verify Your Account">
    Ensure you have a verified tourist account. See our [Tourist Guide](/guides/tourist-guide) for registration steps.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse and Research">
    Explore tours, read descriptions, check guide profiles, and review ratings before booking.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Payment Method">
    Have a valid payment method ready for booking confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Finding the Right Tour

### Using the Explore Page

Start your search on the **Explorar** page:

1. Browse featured experiences in your destination
2. Use the search bar to find specific activities, locations, or guides
3. Filter by category (Gastronomía, Aventura, Historia, Cultura)
4. Read tour cards for quick overviews

### Evaluating Tours

Click on any experience card to view detailed information:

**Tour Details:**
- Complete description of the experience
- Duration and schedule
- Meeting point location
- What's included in the price
- Maximum group size
- Difficulty or intensity level

**Guide Information:**
- Guide's name and profile photo
- Verification badge status
- Years of experience
- Languages spoken
- Certifications and specialties

**Reviews and Ratings:**
- Overall star rating
- Number of reviews
- Recent guest feedback
- Response from the guide

<Tip>
  Look for guides with the verified badge and multiple positive reviews. This indicates a trusted, experienced guide.
</Tip>

### Comparing Options

Save multiple tours to your **Favoritos** to compare:

1. Click the heart icon on tours you're considering
2. Navigate to **Favoritos** from the sidebar
3. Review your saved options side-by-side
4. Make your final selection

## The Booking Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Your Tour">
    Click on the tour you want to book to view full details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Date and Time">
    Select an available date from the guide's calendar. Available dates are highlighted.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Specify Group Size">
    Indicate how many people will attend (up to the maximum group size).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Special Requests">
    Include any special requests, accessibility needs, or questions for the guide.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Booking Details">
    Confirm all information:
    - Tour date and time
    - Number of guests
    - Total price
    - Meeting point
    - Cancellation policy
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Enter payment information and confirm your booking.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive Confirmation">
    You'll receive an email confirmation with all tour details and guide contact information.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Pricing

### Price Components

The total you pay includes:

- **Base tour price** - Per person rate set by the guide
- **Service fee** - Small Kin Conecta platform fee
- **Taxes** - Applicable local taxes

### What's Included

Each tour listing specifies what's included in the price. Common inclusions:

- Guide services
- Entry or admission fees
- Food/drink tastings
- Transportation during the tour
- Equipment or materials
- Photos or souvenirs

<Note>
  Always check the "What's Included" section carefully. Some tours include meals while others don't. Clarify with your guide if anything is unclear.
</Note>

### Additional Costs

Be prepared for possible extras not included:

- Meals or drinks beyond tastings
- Tips for your guide (optional but appreciated)
- Personal shopping or souvenirs
- Additional activities you choose to add

## Booking Confirmation

### What Happens After You Book

1. **Immediate confirmation** - Your booking is confirmed instantly
2. **Email receipt** - Confirmation email with all details
3. **Guide notification** - Your guide is notified of the booking
4. **Added to calendar** - Tour appears in your **Mis viajes** page

### Your Confirmation Includes

- Tour name and description
- Date, time, and duration
- Meeting point with map link
- Guide's name and contact information
- Total price paid
- Cancellation policy
- What to bring or wear

<Tip>
  Save your confirmation email and add the tour to your personal calendar with reminders.
</Tip>

## Managing Your Bookings

### Viewing Upcoming Tours

Navigate to **Mis viajes** to see all your bookings:

- **Featured trip** - Your next upcoming tour displayed prominently
- **Trip history** - All past and future bookings
- **Status indicators** - Confirmed, pending, completed, or cancelled

### Trip Details Page

Click **Ver detalles** on any booking to access:

- Complete tour information
- Guide contact details
- Meeting point with GPS link
- Weather forecast for tour date
- Cancellation options
- Add to calendar button

## Communicating with Your Guide

Before your tour, you may want to contact your guide:

### Using the Chat System

1. From **Mis viajes**, click **Contactar guía** on your booking
2. Or access messages from the chat widget on any page
3. Send your question or request
4. Guide will respond within 24 hours

### What to Ask Your Guide

**Appropriate pre-tour questions:**
- Clarification about meeting point or timing
- Special accommodations or accessibility needs
- What to bring or wear
- Specific interests or preferences
- Dietary restrictions (for food tours)

<Note>
  Keep communication professional and tour-related. The chat system is for coordinating your experience.
</Note>

Learn more in our [Messaging Guide](/guides/messaging).

## Cancellations and Changes

### Cancellation Policy

Each guide sets their own cancellation policy. Common terms:

- **Full refund** - If cancelled 48+ hours before tour
- **Partial refund** - If cancelled 24-48 hours before
- **No refund** - If cancelled less than 24 hours before

### How to Cancel

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Your Booking">
    Go to **Mis viajes** and find the tour you need to cancel.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Request Cancellation">
    Click the cancellation option and confirm your request.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Refund Amount">
    See what refund you'll receive based on timing and policy.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Cancellation">
    Complete the cancellation. You'll receive email confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Requesting Date Changes

To change your tour date:

1. Message your guide through the chat system
2. Request your preferred new date
3. Guide will check availability and confirm
4. If approved, your booking is updated automatically

<Tip>
  Contact your guide as soon as possible if you need to cancel or reschedule. Guides appreciate advance notice and may be more flexible.
</Tip>

### Weather-Related Cancellations

If severe weather makes the tour unsafe:

- Guide may contact you to cancel or reschedule
- Full refund or free rescheduling typically offered
- Check messages and email for guide updates

## Preparing for Your Tour

### Day Before Checklist

- [ ] Review confirmation email
- [ ] Check meeting point location on map
- [ ] Verify tour time (watch for time zone differences)
- [ ] Check weather forecast
- [ ] Prepare items to bring
- [ ] Ensure phone is charged
- [ ] Have guide's contact info accessible

### What to Bring

Common items to pack:

- Confirmation email (digital or printed)
- Valid ID
- Water bottle
- Comfortable walking shoes
- Weather-appropriate clothing
- Camera or phone for photos
- Cash for tips or extras
- Any items specified in tour description

<Note>
  Tour descriptions often include a "What to Bring" section. Check this carefully and pack accordingly.
</Note>

### Arriving at Meeting Point

- Arrive 5-10 minutes early
- Look for your guide (check profile photo)
- Guide may hold a sign with Kin Conecta logo
- If you can't find your guide, call or message them
- Have your booking confirmation ready

## During Your Tour

### Making the Most of Your Experience

- Be present and engaged
- Ask questions - guides love sharing knowledge
- Respect the guide's schedule and timing
- Follow safety instructions
- Be respectful of other group members
- Take photos but don't let it distract from the experience

### If Issues Arise

- Communicate concerns to your guide directly first
- Most issues can be resolved immediately
- For serious problems, contact Kin Conecta support
- Document issues with photos if relevant

## Booking Tips for Best Experience

### Book in Advance

- Popular guides and dates fill quickly
- Book 1-2 weeks ahead when possible
- Last-minute bookings may have limited availability

### Choose the Right Tour

- Match tour difficulty to your fitness level
- Consider group size preferences
- Check duration fits your schedule
- Verify language options

### Communicate Clearly

- Mention special needs or requests upfront
- Ask questions before booking if unsure
- Provide accurate guest count
- Update guide if plans change

### Read Reviews Carefully

- Look for recent reviews (last 3-6 months)
- Read both positive and negative feedback
- See how guides respond to criticism
- Note recurring themes in reviews

<Tip>
  Guides with consistent 5-star reviews and detailed, recent feedback are usually safe bets for quality experiences.
</Tip>

## Payment Security

### Your Payment is Protected

- All transactions secured with encryption
- Payment held until tour completion
- Refund protection for cancellations
- Fraud monitoring on all transactions

### Accepted Payment Methods

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- Digital wallets (varies by region)

<Note>
  Never send payment directly to a guide outside the Kin Conecta platform. All legitimate bookings go through our secure payment system.
</Note>
