## Welcome, Guide!

Kin Conecta empowers local guides to share their expertise and create meaningful connections with travelers. This guide will help you get started and make the most of the platform.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register as a Guide">
    When creating your account, select **Guía** as your account type. Provide:
    - Full name
    - Date of birth (must be 18+)
    - Country code and phone number
    - Email address
    - Strong password (minimum 8 characters)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Profile Setup">
    After registration, complete the profile wizard with information about your guiding experience, specialties, and style.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get Verified">
    Submit required documentation for verification. Once approved, you'll receive the verified guide badge.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The verification process typically takes 2-3 business days. You'll receive an email notification once your account is verified.
</Note>

## Building Your Profile

Your profile is your storefront on Kin Conecta. A complete, compelling profile attracts more bookings.

### Profile Overview

Access your profile from the **Tu perfil** section in the sidebar. Your profile displays:

- **Cover photo** - Showcase your destination or a signature tour moment
- **Profile photo** - A professional, friendly headshot works best
- **Verified badge** - Appears automatically after verification
- **Name and location** - Help travelers find you
- **Star rating** - Average from all your reviews
- **Hourly rate** - Your standard pricing

### About You Section

The **Sobre mí** card is your introduction to travelers:

- Write a compelling summary (2-3 sentences) about who you are
- Share your story - what makes you passionate about guiding?
- Highlight unique expertise or local knowledge
- Keep it authentic and personable

<Tip>
  Travelers connect with guides who show personality. Share what makes you unique - whether it's growing up in the area, a special skill, or deep knowledge of local culture.
</Tip>

### Featured Post

Showcase a highlight from your guiding experience:

- Upload a stunning photo from a recent tour
- Write engaging caption text
- Share an interesting story or insight
- Update regularly to keep your profile fresh

### Guide Style Information

Help travelers understand your approach by completing:

**Style Details:**
- **Estilo** - Your guiding style (storyteller, adventure-focused, educational, etc.)
- **Tamaño del grupo** - Typical group sizes you handle
- **Intensidad del tour** - Physical activity level (relaxed, moderate, intense)
- **Transporte ofrecido** - Whether you provide transportation

**Additional Information:**
- **Estilo con fotos** - Your approach to photography during tours
- **Notas adicionales** - Any special information for travelers

### Quick Summary Sidebar

The mini cards on the right show at-a-glance information:

**Experience Level:**
- Beginner
- Intermediate 
- Expert/Professional

**Group Preferences:**
- Small groups
- Medium groups
- Large groups
- Private tours

**Tour Pace:**
- Relaxed
- Moderate
- Fast-paced

### Locations and Languages

List all locations where you offer tours and languages you speak. This helps tourists find you in searches.

### Certifications and Accessibility

**Upload certifications:**
- First aid/CPR training
- Professional guide licenses
- Specialized certifications (diving, climbing, etc.)
- Language proficiency certificates

**Accessibility adaptations:**
- Wheelchair accessibility
- Accommodations for hearing impairment
- Accommodations for visual impairment
- Other special needs support

<Note>
  Adding certifications and accessibility information builds trust and helps you reach more travelers with diverse needs.
</Note>

### Updating Photos

To update your profile or cover photo:

1. Click the **edit icon** on your photo
2. Select an image from your device
3. Preview the image
4. Click **Guardar imagen** to save

<Tip>
  Use high-quality images that showcase your personality and the experiences you offer. Cover photos work best at landscape orientation.
</Tip>

## Managing Your Dashboard

### Main Dashboard

The **Panel Principal** gives you an overview of:

- Recent bookings and upcoming tours
- New messages from tourists
- Review notifications
- Earnings summary

### Quick Stats

Monitor your performance with key metrics:

- Total active tours
- Upcoming bookings
- Response rate
- Average rating

## Income Tracking

The **Ingresos** page helps you monitor your earnings:

- View earnings by month
- See breakdown by tour type
- Track completed vs. upcoming payments
- Access payment history

## Notifications

Stay on top of your guiding business:

- Click the **notification bell** to view alerts
- Red dot indicates unread notifications
- Get notified about:
  - New booking requests
  - Messages from tourists
  - Reviews from completed tours
  - Payment confirmations

## Best Practices for Success

### Response Time

- Reply to tourist messages within 24 hours
- Quick responses increase booking rates
- Use the chat widget to respond from any page

### Profile Completeness

- Fill out all sections of your profile
- Add multiple high-quality photos
- Keep certifications current
- Update availability regularly

### Tour Quality

- Create detailed, accurate tour descriptions
- Set realistic expectations
- Maintain high standards of service
- Ask satisfied tourists for reviews

<Tip>
  Guides with complete profiles, quick response times, and positive reviews get significantly more bookings. Invest time in these areas!
</Tip>
